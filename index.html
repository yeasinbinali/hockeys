Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ hockey: Rules, equipment, game play explained. Learn about penalties, positions, and key terms. Enhance your hockey knowledge quickly.

What are the basic rules of hockey?

Two teams compete to score goals by hitting a puck into the opponent's net using sticks.

How many players are on a hockey team?

Typically, a team has six players on the ice, including a goalie.

What is offside in hockey?

Offside occurs when an attacking player enters the offensive zone ahead of the puck.

What is icing in hockey?

Icing is when a player shoots the puck from their side of the center line to the opponent's goal line.

How long is a standard hockey game?

In professional hockey, a game consists of three periods, each lasting 20 minutes, with intermissions between.

What is a hat trick in hockey?

A hat trick is when a player scores three goals in a single game.
